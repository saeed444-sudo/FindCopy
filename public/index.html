TOURNAMENT RULES

Eligibility

All participants must be at least 16 years old and have a valid Free Fire account at level 20 or above. Teams must consist of 4 players with one designated captain.

Game Settings

Matches will be played in Classic Mode on Bermuda map. All matches are best-of-one until the quarter finals, which are best-of-three. The grand final is best-of-five.

Code of Conduct

Any form of cheating, hacking, or unsportsmanlike behavior will result in immediate disqualification. Players must maintain respectful communication with opponents and tournament officials.

Registration

Teams must register at least 24 hours before the tournament start time. Registration requires valid contact information for the team captain and all team members.
